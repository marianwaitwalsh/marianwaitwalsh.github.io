Parking: Park nearby on Walton St. or Woodlawn Ave., or in the municipal parking lot between Church St., Woodlawn Ave. and Walton St. (see map).

From the south

  • Take the Northway (I-87) North to Exit 13N.
  • Proceed North on Route 9, 3.9 miles from the traffic light off the Exit 13 ramp at Cherry Choke Rd. into Saratoga Springs. (Route 9 becomes Broadway in Saratoga Springs).
  • Turn left (W) onto Church Street. (The Adirondack Trust Company is on the left (SW) corner, the Post Office is on the NW corner, City Hall is on the NE corner and Phallon Wellness Pharmacy is on the SE corner. Lake Ave. heads E.)
  • Take the 2nd right off Church Street, adjacent to and just past the U.S. Post Office driveway, onto Long Alley, which is one-way North. 42 Long Alley is a two-story brick building, 2nd to the last on the left.

From the north

  • Take the Northway (I-87) South to Exit 15.
  • Take the right Exit ramp onto Route 50, towards Saratoga Springs.
  • Proceed 1.4 miles from the end of the Exit 15 ramp, on Route 50, to the 5th light.
  • At the 5th traffic light, turn left onto Broadway, following signs for Routes 50 and 9 South.
  • After the 1st traffic light, turn right onto Walton Street.
  • Long Alley is the 1st left, but is one way the wrong way. 42 Long Alley is a two-story brick building, 2nd on the right.

FROM THE WEST

  • Take Route 29 to downtown Saratoga Springs (Route 29 becomes Washington Street).
  • Turn left (N) onto Broadway.
  • Turn left (W) onto Church Street. (The Adirondack Trust Company is on the left (SW) corner, the Post Office is on the NW corner, City Hall is on the NE corner and Phallon Wellness Pharmacy is on the SE corner. Lake Avenue heads E.)
  • Take the 2nd right off Church Street, adjacent to and just past the U.S. Post Office driveway, onto Long Alley, which is one- way North. 42 Long Alley is a two- story brick building, 2nd to the last on the left.

From the east

  • Take Route 29 to downtown Saratoga Springs to Broadway (Route 29 becomes Lake Avenue).
  • Continue across Broadway – Lake Avenue becomes Church Street.
  • Take the 2nd right off Church Street, adjacent to and just past the U.S. Post Office driveway, onto Long Alley, which is one- way North. 42 Long Alley is a two- story brick building, 2nd to the last on the left.
